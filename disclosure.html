Transparency Note

Labrador Retriever Care operates under a clear separation between its editorial function and its commercial function. This note documents how that separation works and what income is earned through this site.

Affiliate Income

This site participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. Certain product links within our guides direct visitors to Amazon. If a qualifying purchase is made after following one of these links, Labrador Retriever Care receives a commission from Amazon. This commission is a small percentage of the transaction value and is paid by Amazon — it does not affect the price charged to the buyer.

Research Team Independence

The Lab Care Research Team makes all editorial decisions — including which topics to cover, which products to mention, and what claims to make — without regard to affiliate revenue implications. A topic that generates no affiliate income receives the same editorial attention as one that does. A product that is important for Lab owners is mentioned regardless of whether an affiliate link is available.

No brand has a paid relationship with Labrador Retriever Care. No manufacturer pays to have their products included in, excluded from, or positively described in our guides. The Research Team's positions are earned through accuracy, not purchased.

Legal Notice

This disclosure satisfies the requirements of 16 CFR Part 255 (Federal Trade Commission guidelines on endorsements). Labrador Retriever Care is a participant in the Amazon Associates Programme. As an Amazon Associate, this site earns from qualifying purchases.

No Paid Content

Labrador Retriever Care does not accept payment for editorial coverage. There are no sponsored posts, advertorials, or paid product placements on this site. Every guide is produced under the sole editorial authority of the Lab Care Research Team and meets the Content Principles described separately.
